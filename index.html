How to Get Your Instagram Data

To use this tool, you first need to request your "Followers and following" data from Instagram.

  1. Go to your profile and tap the Menu icon (☰) in the top right.
  2. Select "Your activity".
  3. Tap "Download your information", then "Download or transfer information".
  4. Choose "Some of your information".
  5. Select only "Followers and following" from the list and tap "Next".
  6. Choose "Download to device" and tap "Next".
  7. Set the format to JSON and the date range to All time. This is important!
  8. Tap "Save". Instagram will email you a link to a `.zip` file when it's ready.

Once you have the `.zip` file, you can upload it here directly using Option A.
